> [!NOTE]
> **Fashion** is a form of self-expression with a specific context, such as time, place and purpose. Example of these are clothing, footwear, lifestyle, accessories, makeup, hairstyle, and body posture. The term implies a look defined by the fashion industry as that which is _trending_. Everything that is considered _fashion_ is available and popularized by the fashion system.(https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fashion)
> 
> **Textile arts** are arts and crafts that use plant, animal, or synthetic fibers to construct practical or decorative objects. (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Textile_arts)
